Impermanence, also known as the philosophical problem of change, is a philosophical concept addressed in a variety of religions and philosophies. In Eastern philosophy, it is the three marks of existence. It is also an element of Hinduism. In the West, the writings of Heraclitus introduced the concept.

Dealing With Loss

1. Dealing With Loss

There is no right or wrong way to grieve, but there are healthy ways to deal with the grieving process. Grief is a natural response to loss. Ways to deal with loss:

  1. Acknowledge your pain.
  2. Accept that grief triggers emotions.
  3. Seek out face-to-face support.
  4. Exercise, it helps with overthinking.

Building Confidence

2. Building Confidence

Self-confidence is not a static measure. Our confidence can increase and decrease depending on many factors like mood. Ways to Improve Confidence:

  1. Always plan and prepare.
  2. Keep learning and gaining knowledge.
  3. Try to think positively.
  4. Get out of your comfort zone more.

Love, Love, Love

3. Love, Love, Love

Everyone wants to feel deeply connected to other people, fully seen and appreciated by them, and secure in those relationships. Ways to create more love:

  1. Start meaningful chats.
  2. Gift your presence.
  3. Open your mind.
  4. Value everyone near you.
